The Atlanta region is taking active steps to plan for the rapid advancements in transportation technology.

Join us for the ConnectATL Summit to learn from industry leaders and local governments about the opportunities and challenges to come.

The ConnectATL Summit is designed for local and state government staff and elected officials, regional transportation experts, and industry leaders, to provide an opportunity to come together and learn from each other about the technologies, products, and policies that will lead to a faster, smarter implementation of the latest advances and innovations in transportation and mobility.
